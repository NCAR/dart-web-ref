Profiles of atmospheric quantities deduced from GPS Radio Occultation data are available in otherwise data-sparse regions and provide information used to forecast the behavior of tropical cyclones. The [COSMIC/FORMOSAT-3]("http://www.cosmic.ucar.edu/") mission has been providing about 2000 data profiles per day since September 2007.
